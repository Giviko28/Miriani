SILKNET LLC — ENTERPRISE CLIENT ONBOARDING PLAYBOOK
Account Management & NOC Operations | Rev 2.0 | February 2025
INTERNAL — Account Managers, TAMs, NOC Engineers

This playbook defines the end-to-end process for activating a new Enterprise or
Platinum corporate client. It covers provisioning, NOC integration, account setup,
and the kickoff handover. Use Miriani to generate the onboarding task list for a
specific client: "Onboard new client [Company Name], [service], starting [date]."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PHASE 1 — CONTRACT & COMMERCIAL (Week -2 before go-live)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
□ Signed contract received by Sales Operations (sales-ops@silknet.com)
□ Contract uploaded to CRM (Salesforce, opportunity stage → Closed Won)
□ Account Manager assigns Tier (Silver / Gold / Platinum) based on contract value
□ TAM assigned (Platinum only): TAM intro email sent to client within 24 hours
□ Billing contact and technical contact collected from client:
    - Billing contact: name, email, Georgian TIN (SAIDENTIFIKACIO NOMERI), address for VAT invoice
    - Technical contact: name, email, mobile (for NOC to call during incidents)
□ First invoice raised by Finance for setup fee (if applicable)
□ Account created in Billing system (billing-admin@silknet.com)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PHASE 2 — NETWORK PROVISIONING (Week -1)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
□ NOC provisioning ticket created in Jira (project: PROV, type: New Corporate Circuit)
  Required: client name, site address, service type, bandwidth, requested go-live date,
  CPE model (Silknet-provided or client-managed with BGP details).
□ Field survey completed (if new fibre pull required): survey team booked via Jira FIELD project
□ IP addressing allocated:
    - Public static IPs: /29 block standard for Enterprise; /28 for Platinum
    - BGP AS number assigned (if client-managed CPE)
    - Allocation logged in NetBox (netbox.silknet.ge) by NOC engineer
□ CPE configured and staged:
    - Huawei AR651 pre-configured with client's IP block, default route, QoS policy
    - CPE labelled with client name, circuit ID, and NOC contact sticker
□ Physical installation:
    - Field crew dispatched to client site; SLA: 5 business days from provisioning ticket creation
    - On-site: CPE racked, fiber terminated, IP connectivity verified end-to-end
    - Field crew closes Jira ticket with sign-off photo attached

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PHASE 3 — NOC MONITORING INTEGRATION (Go-live day)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
□ Client added to Zabbix monitoring:
    - Host created under group "Corporate-[Tier]"
    - Ping, SNMP, bandwidth utilisation, interface status templates applied
    - Alert thresholds: >80 % utilisation for 5 min → P3 alert; interface down → P2 (Enterprise) or P1 (Platinum)
□ PagerDuty escalation rule configured for this client (Platinum only: direct page to TAM)
□ Circuit ID registered in the network inventory (Atlas, under Corporate Circuits)
□ NOC team briefed: client name, tier, contacts, and any special handling notes added to
  the NOC "hot clients" runbook (noc-wiki.silknet.ge/hot-clients)
□ Bandwidth test run from NOC: confirm full contracted speed, latency <5 ms to Tbilisi IX

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PHASE 4 — CLIENT KICKOFF & HANDOVER (Go-live day + 1)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
□ Kickoff call with client: Account Manager + TAM (Platinum) / AM (Enterprise)
  Agenda: confirm service is live, introduce support channels, review SLA, share escalation contacts
□ Welcome pack sent to client (email):
    - Circuit ID and IP details PDF
    - NOC contact details (24/7 number, email)
    - Silknet client portal login (portal.silknet.ge) — for real-time bandwidth graphs, ticket submission
    - TAM's direct mobile (Platinum only)
    - SLA summary: uptime commitment, credit process, maintenance window schedule
□ First monthly report scheduled:
    - Platinum: monthly SLA report auto-generated by NOC system, sent by TAM on 5th of each month
    - Enterprise: quarterly report; AM sends summary
□ Account Manager updates Salesforce: stage → Active Customer; next review date set for 90 days

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PHASE 5 — 30-DAY CHECK-IN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
□ Account Manager calls client at day 30 to confirm satisfaction
□ Review NOC logs: any incidents, SLA breaches, or high utilisation spikes during first 30 days
□ If utilisation consistently >70 %: proactively recommend bandwidth upgrade (upsell opportunity)
□ NPS survey sent to client technical and billing contacts
