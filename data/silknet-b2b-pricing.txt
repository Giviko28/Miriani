1. DEDICATED FIBER INTERNET (DIA — Dedicated Internet Access)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All prices are monthly recurring, in GEL, excluding VAT (18 %).
Symmetrical bandwidth, uncontended, SLA tier assigned per contract value (see SLA doc).

  100 Mbps      350 GEL / month
  200 Mbps      600 GEL / month
  500 Mbps      900 GEL / month
  1 Gbps      1,500 GEL / month
  2 Gbps      2,600 GEL / month
  10 Gbps     8,000 GEL / month
  10 Gbps (redundant dual-path)  14,000 GEL / month

Setup / installation fee: Equal to one month's recurring fee.
Waived for: contracts ≥ 24 months, or contract value > 18,000 GEL/year.

CPE (router provided by Silknet): 0 GEL on 12-month+ contracts (Huawei AR651).
Client-managed CPE: BGP hand-off available; AS64512 range allocated by NOC.

2. MPLS / PRIVATE WAN (MULTI-SITE)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Base hub site (Tbilisi):        200 GEL / month
Each additional spoke site:     150 GEL / month per site
Bandwidth per site:             Priced per DIA table above

Example — Tegeta Motors (8 showrooms + head office):
  Hub (HQ, 1 Gbps):       1,500 GEL
  8 spokes (100 Mbps each): 8 × (350 + 150) = 4,000 GEL
  Total recurring:         5,500 GEL / month (+ VAT)

QoS classes available: 4 (Voice priority, Business Critical, Best Effort, Bulk).
Minimum contract: 12 months.

3. DATA CENTRE & COLOCATION (TBILISI DC — TSERETELI)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The Tbilisi DC is Tier III certified (99.98 % uptime). Power redundancy: 2N UPS + diesel.

  1U rack unit:          80 GEL / month
  Quarter rack (10U):   600 GEL / month
  Half rack (22U):    1,100 GEL / month
  Full rack (42U):    2,000 GEL / month
  Full rack + KVM:    2,200 GEL / month

Power:
  Included: 2 kW per full rack (shared circuit).
  Dedicated 16A circuit: +200 GEL / month.
  Dedicated 32A circuit: +380 GEL / month.

Cross-connects (within DC):
  Copper (Cat6):        50 GEL / month per port pair
  Fiber (LC/SM):       100 GEL / month per port pair

Remote hands: 80 GEL per 30 min during business hours; 150 GEL per 30 min after hours.
