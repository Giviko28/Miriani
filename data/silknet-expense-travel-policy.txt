SILKNET LLC — EXPENSE & BUSINESS TRAVEL POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective: 1 January 2025
Confidential — For Internal Use Only

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. PURPOSE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This policy governs how Silknet employees incur, document, and recover business
expenses. All expenditure must be necessary, reasonable, and in the best interests
of Silknet. Employees who abuse this policy will be subject to disciplinary action
and may be required to repay funds.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. SUBMITTING AN EXPENSE REPORT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Deadline: Expense reports must be submitted within 10 working days of the expense
being incurred. Late submissions require VP-level approval and may be denied.

How to submit:
  1. Log in to the Silknet HR Portal (hr.silknet.ge).
  2. Navigate to Finance → Expense Claims → New Claim.
  3. Enter each expense line: date, category, amount (GEL), description.
  4. Attach a photo or scan of the original receipt for every item above 20 GEL.
  5. Submit for line-manager approval.

Alternatively, ask Miriani: "I need to submit an expense report for [details]"
and Miriani will draft the claim form for you.

Approval chain:
  • Up to 500 GEL   → Line manager
  • 501–2,000 GEL   → Line manager + department head
  • Above 2,000 GEL → Line manager + department head + Finance Director

Reimbursement: Approved claims are paid via bank transfer to the employee's salary
account on the 15th of the following month.

Receipts: Original receipts must be kept by the employee for 12 months after
reimbursement in case of audit. Digital scans are acceptable for items under 500 GEL.

3. ELIGIBLE EXPENSE CATEGORIES & LIMITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Category                 | Limit per occasion        | Notes
─────────────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────────
Business meals           | 80 GEL per person         | Alcohol capped at 20 GEL/person
Client entertainment     | 200 GEL per person        | Pre-approval required; max 8 people
Taxi / ride-share        | Actual cost               | Uber/Bolt receipt required
Parking                  | 15 GEL per day            | Tbilisi city centre only
Mobile top-up (roaming)  | 50 GEL per trip           | Non-Silknet SIM only
Office supplies          | 100 GEL per month         | Receipts required above 20 GEL
Training materials       | 300 GEL per course        | Pre-approved by HR
Gifts to clients         | 50 GEL per recipient      | Must be branded Silknet merchandise

Personal expenses (groceries, personal clothing, gym, etc.) are NOT reimbursable.

4. DOMESTIC BUSINESS TRAVEL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Travel to another Silknet regional office or customer site within Georgia:

Daily per diem (meals & incidentals): 60 GEL per day
Accommodation cap: 150 GEL per night (single room)
Transport: Train (1st class) or minibus; private taxi pre-approved for locations with
no public transport.

Trips must be logged in the HR portal under Travel Requests at least 3 working days
in advance.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS TRAVEL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All international travel requires approval from the employee's department head and
a completed Travel Request Form submitted at least 10 working days before departure.

Daily per diem by region:
  EU / UK / US / UAE: 120 USD equivalent
  CIS countries (Armenia, Azerbaijan, Turkey): 80 USD equivalent
  Other: 90 USD equivalent

Flight class: Economy for flights under 5 hours; Business class permitted for flights
exceeding 5 hours or if the employee must work immediately upon arrival. Silknet's
preferred booking platform is Booking.com Corporate (access via the intranet).

Accommodation cap: 200 USD per night (up to 3 stars; 4-star if client-facing trip).

Travel insurance: Silknet provides automatic Aldagi corporate travel insurance for all
approved business trips. Employees must register the trip in the HR portal to activate
coverage.

6. CORPORATE CREDIT CARD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Corporate TBC Visa cards are issued to department heads and employees who travel more
than 4 times per year. Card limit: 3,000 GEL/month by default (adjustable with CFO
approval). Monthly statements must be reconciled within 5 working days of month end.
Personal purchases on the corporate card are a disciplinary offence.

7. NON-COMPLIANT CLAIMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Finance may reject any claim that lacks receipts, exceeds policy limits without
prior approval, or is submitted after the 10-day deadline. Rejected claims are
returned with a written reason. The employee may resubmit with a VP exception
approval within 5 working days.
