1. SCOPE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This policy applies to all Silknet employees, contractors, and third-party vendors
who access Silknet systems, networks, or data. Non-compliance may result in
disciplinary action, contract termination, and legal liability.

2. HOW TO REQUEST IT EQUIPMENT OR ACCESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
New equipment or system access requests:
  1. Submit a ticket via the Miriani AI assistant ("I need a new laptop" or
     "I need access to the billing system") — Miriani will route this to IT Helpdesk
     and create a Jira ticket automatically.
  2. Alternatively, email it-helpdesk@silknet.com with your employee ID in the subject.
  3. Standard SLA: laptops and peripherals — 3 working days. Software licences — 1 day.
     Network access provisioning — 2 hours for standard roles, up to 2 days for
     elevated/privileged access requiring Security team approval.

Loaner equipment for travel is available from the IT Asset store on Floor 2 (Tbilisi HQ).
Reserve at least 2 working days in advance via the Helpdesk.

3. PASSWORD POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All Silknet accounts (Windows AD, email, Jira, VPN, cloud portals) must use:
  • Minimum 12 characters
  • At least one uppercase, one lowercase, one digit, one special character
  • No reuse of last 10 passwords
  • Password rotation every 90 days (enforced by AD policy)

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for:
  • VPN access
  • Microsoft 365 / email
  • Jira and Confluence
  • AWS and Azure consoles
  • The Silknet Customer Data Platform

Password managers: Silknet licenses 1Password for all employees. Setup instructions
are on the intranet (IT Guides → 1Password). Use of personal password managers is
discouraged but not prohibited.

Sharing passwords is strictly prohibited and is grounds for disciplinary action.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. VPN USAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The Silknet corporate VPN (Cisco AnyConnect) must be active whenever accessing any
internal system remotely — including email, Jira, ERP, or HR portal.

VPN tokens are issued by the IT Security team. If your token is lost or stolen, report
it immediately to it-security@silknet.com and call +995 32 222 0100 ext. 1099.

VPN split-tunneling is disabled; all traffic is routed through Silknet's security
gateways when the VPN is active.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. ACCEPTABLE USE OF COMPANY DEVICES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Company laptops, phones, and SIM cards are provided for business use. Incidental
personal use is tolerated but must not:
  • Install unauthorized software (only Software Center / approved app catalogue)
  • Disable antivirus (Sophos Endpoint) or firewall
  • Connect to public Wi-Fi without VPN active
  • Store customer data on personal cloud services (Google Drive, personal Dropbox, etc.)
  • Access adult content, gambling, or illegal material

Company devices are subject to remote monitoring and may be wiped remotely if lost,
stolen, or if the employee is under investigation.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6. DATA CLASSIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Public       — Marketing materials, press releases.
Internal     — Internal memos, project plans. Do not share externally without approval.
Confidential — Customer data, financial records, network maps. Encrypt in transit and
               at rest; share only on need-to-know basis.
Restricted   — Signing keys, authentication secrets, M&A data. Access requires CISO
               sign-off; must never leave Silknet-controlled systems.

7. REPORTING SECURITY INCIDENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Report immediately if you:
  • Suspect your account has been compromised
  • Receive a suspicious phishing email
  • Lose or have a device stolen
  • Observe unusual activity on network equipment

How to report:
  • Via Miriani: "I think I have a security incident" — creates a P1 Jira ticket.
  • Email: it-security@silknet.com (monitored 24/7)
  • Phone: Security hotline +995 32 222 0100 ext. 1099 (24/7)

Never attempt to investigate or resolve a security incident yourself — always escalate
to the Security team.
