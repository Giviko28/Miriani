1. HEALTH INSURANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Provider: ABA Insurance (Group Corporate Plan — "Gold" tier)

Coverage (effective from first day of employment):
  • Outpatient consultations: 100 % covered at Aversi, Evex, and Curatio network clinics
  • Emergency inpatient: 100 % covered at any licensed Georgian hospital
  • Planned inpatient surgery: 90 % covered (10 % co-pay per episode, capped at 500 GEL/year)
  • Dental: Up to 400 GEL per calendar year (fillings, extractions, cleaning)
  • Vision: Up to 200 GEL per calendar year (glasses or lenses)
  • Mental health: 6 sessions per year covered (psychologist, psychiatrist)
  • Medications: 70 % reimbursement on prescription drugs, up to 300 GEL/year

Family coverage: After completing 12 months of service, employees may add a spouse
and up to 2 children (under 18) to the plan. The company covers 70 % of the family
premium; the employee's contribution is deducted from salary.

How to use: Show your Silknet employee ID card at any Aversi or Evex reception. For
reimbursement outside the network, submit receipts to hr@silknet.com within 30 days.

2. GYM & WELLNESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Silknet has a corporate partnership with SportLife (all Tbilisi branches) and
Active+ (Kutaisi, Batumi). Employees receive a 40 % discount on any monthly
membership plan.

To activate: Email hr@silknet.com with your employee ID. The discount code is
sent within 1 working day.

Silknet HQ (Tsereteli 18, Block B, 4th floor) has an in-office gym with cardio
equipment and free weights, open Monday–Friday 07:00–21:00, and Saturday 09:00–15:00.
Access is via employee badge. No booking required for the main floor; book group
classes (yoga, HIIT, pilates) on the intranet (Wellness → Class Bookings).

3. SILKNET SERVICE DISCOUNTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All permanent Silknet employees are entitled to the following personal-use discounts:

  • Silknet Home Fiber Internet: 50 % off any residential plan (up to 1 Gbps)
  • Silknet Mobile SIM: Free "Employee 30" plan — 30 GB data, unlimited calls & SMS
    within Georgia, 300 international minutes per month
  • Silknet TV (IPTV): 2 premium bouquets free of charge
  • Silknet Cloud Storage (10 GB): Free for all employees

To activate home internet or TV discount: contact hr@silknet.com with your home
address and desired plan. Activation within 5 working days.

4. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Annual learning budget: Each employee receives 600 GEL per calendar year for
external training, online courses, certifications, or professional conferences.
Budget resets on 1 January and cannot be carried over.

Eligible spend: Udemy, Coursera, LinkedIn Learning subscriptions; industry
certifications (Cisco CCNA/CCNP, AWS, PMI, ITIL, Google, etc.); relevant
conference registration fees.

How to use:
  1. Request approval from your line manager (email is sufficient).
  2. Purchase the course/certification.
  3. Submit the receipt under the "Training" expense category on the HR portal.

Language courses: Silknet funds English, Russian, and Turkish language courses at
the IH Tbilisi Language Centre (50 % company-paid, 50 % employee contribution).
Enrolment is once per year in September; apply via HR.

Internal Silknet Academy: Free access to all instructor-led and e-learning courses
on the Silknet intranet (Learning → Academy). Topics include telecom fundamentals,
leadership, project management, cybersecurity, and customer service.

5. PARKING & COMMUTE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Tbilisi HQ (Tsereteli 18): 120 reserved underground parking spaces allocated by
seniority and distance from office. Waiting list managed by Facilities. To apply:
facilities@silknet.com.

Company shuttle: A free shuttle bus runs twice daily on the Saburtalo ↔ HQ ↔
Isani ↔ Varketili corridor (morning 08:30 pickup; evening 18:15 from HQ). Route
and schedule on the intranet (Commute → Shuttle).

Metro subsidy: Employees without parking or shuttle access receive a monthly
metro card (loaded with 40 GEL) via HR.

Silknet partners with Mindscape (mindscape.ge) to offer all employees and immediate
family members free, confidential support:
  • 6 free counselling sessions per year (in-person or video)
  • Legal consultation (1 hour per quarter)
  • Financial planning consultation (1 session per year)

Access is fully confidential — HR and management are never informed. Contact
Mindscape directly at +995 32 200 8080 or eap@mindscape.ge.
